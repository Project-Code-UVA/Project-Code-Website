Project Code

About Us

Mission statement:

Project Code aims to empower UVA students by emphasizing a problem-solving process through real-life applications of computer science. Connecting passionate individuals with various skills and similar interests presents a high priority. Numerous students have never attempted a CS project before, and we hope to offer them the opportunity to prepare for life outside of UVA. These projects will provide students with the necessary skills and a creative outlet.
